Welcome to ealth-Foresight

Diabetes and heart diseases are common worldwide, affecting all age groups. Diabetes impairs blood sugar control, raising cardiovascular disease risk and damaging heart blood vessels.

Early detection can prevent complications. An all-in-one web app predicting these conditions and offering Prediction, precautions and treatments are beneficial, providing with proactive health management tools.

The Health-Foresight helps to identify risks, offering preventive measures and treatment options, guiding you the diagnosed. We aims to improve health management and reduce the global impact of these diseases.
